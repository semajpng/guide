Build Guide: MX (Top Mount)

1 ✱ Assemble switches onto the PC plate of your choosing (ANSI/ISO)
2 ✱ Mount the plate and switches onto the top case
3 ✱ Assemble your stabs onto your MX/ALPS PCB
4 ✱ Solder your top case assembly onto the PCB
5 ✱ Enjoy. If you need to disassemble, desolder first!

Build Guide: MX (O-Ring Mount)

1 ✱ Assemble stabs onto your MX/ALPS PCB
2 ✱ Solder on your switches. Plate is optional.
3 ✱ Install ⌀3mm gummy snek onto top case (F1 style)
4 ✱ Install ⌀2.7mm gummy snek between PCB assembly and bottom case
5 ✱ Screw the top case and bottom case together!
