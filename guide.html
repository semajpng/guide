Included in the box, you will find

- 1 x JE-BAE-TED keybort (purple top, brass bottom)
- 2 x MX/ALPS Solder PCBs
- 2 x EC PCBs
- 1 x ANSI EC Aluminum Plate
- 1 x ANSI MX PC Plate
- 1 x ISO MX PC Plate
- 1 x ALPS PC Plate
- 1 x ⌀3mm Silicon Gummy Snek
- 1 x ⌀2.7mm Silicon Gummy Snek
- 1 x MX DCS BAE (black)
- 1 x ALPS Taihao Cubic BAE (beige)
- 5 x ALPS Switches (clones)
- 1 x ALPS Stab kit

Keybort Specs

- 9 Degree Typing Angle
- AFH: 21.4 mm
- Weight: 180g unbuilt
- Mounting method: O-ring / Top Mount
- Switch Support: MX / ALPS / EC

Layout Support

- Supports ANSI enter + 1u
- Supports Numpad enter + 1u
- Supports BAE
link

Build Guide: MX (Top Mount)

Step 1: Assemble switches onto the PC plate of your choosing (ANSI/ISO)
Step 2: Mount the plate and switches onto the top case
Step 3: Assemble your stabs onto your MX/ALPS PCB
Step 4: Solder your top case assembly onto the PCB
Step 5: Enjoy. If you need to disassemble, desolder first!

Build Guide: MX (O-Ring Mount)

Step 1: Assemble stabs onto your MX/ALPS PCB
Step 2: Solder on your switches. Plate is optional.
Step 3: Install ⌀3mm gummy snek onto top case (F1 style)
Step 4: Install ⌀2.7mm gummy snek between PCB assembly and bottom case
Step 5: Screw the top case and bottom case together!
